Welcome

This final version of our Green Computing website includes real strategies for reducing the environmental impact of technology in schools, homes, and companies across South Africa.

Focus on Schools

Schools in South Africa can go green by reducing paper use, promoting digital submissions, using laptops over desktops, and teaching digital sustainability in subjects like CAT and Life Orientation. Recycling bins, unplugged devices, and classroom energy-saving policies make a real impact.
