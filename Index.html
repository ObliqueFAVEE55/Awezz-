Welcome

This website is created by Tebogo Selae Mphosi as part of the Grade 10 CAT PAT 2025. It explores how schools, people, and companies can reduce the environmental impact of technology through green computing.

Green Computing in Schools

  • Use laptops instead of power-hungry desktops
  • Enable power-saving settings on all school devices
  • Encourage students to submit digital assignments
  • Recycle or donate unused tech equipment
  • Teach environmental IT practices in CAT, LO and NS

How People Can Help

  • Turn off electronics when not in use
  • Unplug chargers to avoid phantom power usage
  • Reduce screen brightness to save battery and power
  • Use energy-efficient devices and extend their lifespan

What Companies Are Doing

Leading tech companies are working toward sustainability:

  • MTN: Recycles e-waste from clients and supports solar-powered towers
  • HP: Offers cartridge recycling and carbon-neutral printing
  • Vodacom: Runs green base stations and recycles phones
